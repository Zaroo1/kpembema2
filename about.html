About Our School

Our History

KPEMBE M/A Junior High School was established in 1957 with the mission to provide quality education to the children of Kpembe and surrounding communities. Over the years, we have grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the Savannah Region.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that develops students academically, morally, and socially, preparing them to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that nurtures talents and potentials of students for national and global impact.

Our Facilities

  • Well-equipped classrooms
  • Science laboratory
  • Computer lab
  • Library
  • Sports field
